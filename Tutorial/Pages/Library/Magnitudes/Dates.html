Dates
Dates are implemented by the class: Date .
Date objects also store the time, in millisecond precision.
Date new creates a new date with the current date and time.
Date now returns the number of milliseconds elapsed since January 1st, 1970, UTC.
This can be used for calculating time differences.
Dates can be created through their date and time component values:
Year, month, day, hours, minutes, seconds.
These component values can also be retrieved from date objects.
(The month and day numbers in dates are 1-based, not 0-based.)
Dates are timezone aware.

Examples:
  • Date new returns e.g. Sun Jan 25 2026 20:38:31 GMT+0100 ... .
  • Date now returns e.g. 1769369979237 .
  • Date year: 2026 month: 1 day: 2 returns Fri Jan 02 2026 00:00:00 ... .
  • ( Date fromString: '1970-01-02' ) year returns 1970 .
